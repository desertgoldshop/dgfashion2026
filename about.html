About Desert Gold

A wholesale catalog built for reseller testing.

Desert Gold is a developing B2B wholesale jewelry catalog for resellers, boutiques, online sellers and event buyers. We keep active styles visible, use product IDs for quoting, and support mixed-style test orders.

Our approach

Show the product. Explain the process.

Each active style has its own product page, product ID, sample price and direct inquiry path. Buyers can request current photos, material notes, stock and bulk pricing before confirming an order.

We publish the practical details wholesale buyers care about: MOQ testing, mixed styles, payment confirmation, shipping confirmation, damage reporting and return authorization.

Order rules

How wholesale orders are handled.

Use these rules as the default. Special requests can be confirmed in writing before payment.

MOQ

Low MOQ testing is supported where stock allows. First buyers can send a small mixed-style list for quotation before scaling quantity.

Mixed styles

Mixed styles are accepted. Send product IDs, quantity for each style and the destination country to receive a clean quote.

Sample price

Public prices are sample screening prices in USD. They help buyers compare styles quickly; they are not final landed wholesale costs.

Bulk price

Ask for 20, 50 or 100+ piece quantity quotes. Final pricing depends on product availability, quantity, packing and shipping.

Packing

Simple protective packing is the default. Gift boxes, labels or special packing can be quoted separately if available.

Shipping

Shipping method, cost and estimate depend on destination, quantity and package weight. These are confirmed before payment.

Payment

PayPal payment is used only after product IDs, quantity, shipping and final total are confirmed in writing.

After-sales

For damaged or incorrect items, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with product ID, package photo and item photos.

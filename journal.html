Gifting

Choosing a jewelry gift for a Saudi occasion

Start with the person and how they dress. A smaller pendant or fine chain suits frequent wear; a brighter pendant or coordinated selection works better for an occasion. Keep the style ID and ask for current photos before deciding.

Care

Caring for gold-tone fashion jewelry

Keep pieces dry and separate. Apply perfume and lotion before wearing jewelry, then wipe the piece gently after use. Avoid harsh cleaners and store the item away from humidity and direct sunlight.

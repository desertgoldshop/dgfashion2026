Shipping & Returns

Last updated July 11, 2026. Written for practical clarity before and after an order.

Before payment

We confirm the style ID, quantity, delivery city, item total, shipping estimate and available delivery method.

Dispatch and tracking

When tracking is available, it is shared after dispatch. Timing can be affected by customs and local carriers.

Order changes

Contact us immediately if an order needs to change. Changes are not guaranteed after preparation or dispatch begins.

Damaged or incorrect items

Email within 48 hours of delivery with the style ID, packaging photo and clear item photos for review.

Return authorization

Do not send an item back without written authorization. The return address and available resolution are confirmed case by case.

Resolution

After review, an eligible issue may be resolved through replacement, partial refund or refund, depending on the order and evidence.

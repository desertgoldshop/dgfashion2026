Product, shipping and payment details should be clear before a buyer commits.

No. This collection focuses on fashion jewelry and gold-tone styles. Current material details are confirmed before an order.

Can I request current product photos?

Yes. Send the style ID and we will share the available photos, details and current availability.

How is shipping calculated?

Shipping depends on destination and order quantity. The method, estimate and cost are confirmed before payment.

Yes. Saudi Arabia is the first market focus, with GCC inquiries also welcome.

PayPal checkout is available after order confirmation. Email the style ID, quantity and destination first so the product total and shipping are confirmed before payment.

Contact us immediately. Changes are not guaranteed after preparation or dispatch begins.
